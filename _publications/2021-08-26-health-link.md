This paper proposes a hybrid scalable health information exchange platform in Thailand. Such system expedites the process of referrel, general treatment, and safety in emergency cases. The results show that interoperability, privacy, and security can be implemented with  Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources (FHIR), pseudonymization, and access control respectively.
